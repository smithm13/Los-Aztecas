About Us

Los Aztecas is a family-owned business. We as a family and a restaurant have an Aztec influence. Our home state in Mexico, Jalisco, is known for sombreros, rodeos, the Mexican Hat Dance, mariachi music, and is the birthplace of tequila. Where we come from it is common to own a Mexican restaurant.
